About Us

Specialty Chemical Solutions for Industrial Applications

Our Story: A Legacy of Innovation

Founded in 2009, Unistar has been dedicated to providing well-performing, high-quality, and sustainable chemical products to meet the increasing needs of a developing global chemical industry. As a leading chemical manufacturer of basic and intermediate chemicals, our products portfolio includes solvents, foam blowing agents, surfactants, and specialty products for pharmaceuticals. Today, Unistar continues to expand its product portfolio while providing dependable chemical solutions that help customers meet evolving industry challenges.

Commitment to Sustainability

At Unistar Chemical, we are committed to developing innovative specialty chemical solutions that support both performance and sustainability. Through continuous product development and responsible manufacturing practices, we help customers adapt to evolving industry demands while delivering reliable, high-quality products for the future. By investing in innovative technologies and next-generation products, we are helping build a more sustainable future for the chemical industry.
